# Revenue Recognition Policy
## Company Financial Policy v2.1

---

[CITE=POL-001]
## 1. General Revenue Recognition Principles
Revenue shall be recognized when the following criteria are ALL satisfied:
- Delivery of goods or services is complete
- Customer acceptance has been obtained (explicit or implied)
- The transaction price is determinable
- Collection is reasonably assured

---

[CITE=POL-002]
## 2. Software License Revenue
For software license arrangements:
- Revenue may be recognized upon delivery if no significant post-delivery obligations exist
- If customer acceptance is contractually required, revenue recognition must wait until acceptance is obtained
- Multi-element arrangements must allocate revenue based on standalone selling prices

---

[CITE=POL-003]
## 3. Service Revenue Recognition  
For service-based revenue:
- Revenue is recognized over the service period
- Milestone-based recognition is permitted when milestones are clearly defined
- Early termination clauses must be evaluated for revenue reversal risk

---

[CITE=POL-004]
## 4. Documentation Requirements
The following documentation is REQUIRED for revenue recognition:
- Signed customer acceptance or UAT signoff
- Delivery confirmation or go-live evidence
- Invoice with matching PO number
- Evidence that any termination windows have expired or been waived

Failure to provide documentation may result in deferred recognition.
